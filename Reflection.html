4th Quarter Reflection

This marks the final quarter of ICT, and I can't say that I didn't enjoy it. ICT has started to become my favorite subject since the grade 9 level started, and with Mrs. Uminga's teaching, I became even more invested, leaning even closer into the possibility of my future of becoming a computer science major.

I enjoyed this quarter, and all the other prequisite ones. Thank you so much Mrs. Uminga, for being one of the most enjoyable ICT teachers ever since grade 7. I, and Faith, love you so much!!
